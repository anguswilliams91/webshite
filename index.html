About me

I'm a final year Ph.D. student in Astrophysics at the University of Cambridge. My thesis has focused using the dynamics of old stars in the Milky Way to infer the spatial distribution of dark matter. I build statistical models of these stars and compare them to measurements from large scale surveys of the Galaxy. I write software in a mix of Python, C and C++, and occassionally Mathematica.
